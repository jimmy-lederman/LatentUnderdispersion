Data Dictionary: Finnish Bird Abundance Data
=============================================

Source: Finnish bird monitoring survey data.

Files:
------

1. birds_final_counts.csv
   - Dimensions: 2826 rows (survey sites) x 137 columns (species)
   - Each column is a bird species (scientific name, e.g., "Anthus trivialis", "Fringilla coelebs")
   - Values: integer abundance counts observed at each site
   - First column: row index

2. birds_final_covariates.csv
   - Dimensions: 2826 rows (survey sites) x 21 columns (covariates)
   - First column: row index
   - Columns:
     - (Intercept): intercept term (all 1s)
     - Mixed_Forest: proportion of mixed forest land cover
     - Deciduous_Forest: proportion of deciduous forest land cover
     - Shrubs: proportion of shrub land cover
     - Grasslands_Wetlands: proportion of grasslands/wetlands land cover
     - Acricultural_Land: proportion of agricultural land cover
     - Barren: proportion of barren land cover
     - Urban: proportion of urban land cover
     - Water_Bodies: proportion of water body land cover
     - Coastal: coastal indicator
     - Stand_Age: forest stand age
     - Pine_Volume: pine tree volume
     - Spruce_Volume: spruce tree volume
     - Birch_Volume: birch tree volume
     - Other_Deciduous_Volume: other deciduous tree volume
     - VMI_PC1: first principal component of VMI (Finnish forest inventory) variables
     - duration: survey duration
     - linelength: survey transect line length
     - polyAprMay1: polynomial basis for April-May timing
     - polyDecFeb1: polynomial basis for December-February timing
     - polyJunJul1: polynomial basis for June-July timing

3. birds_final_names.csv
   - Dimensions: 137 rows (species) x 2 columns
   - Columns:
     - index: integer index for each species
     - species: scientific name of the species

Data Dictionary: COVID-19 Cumulative Case Counts
=================================================

Source: https://github.com/nytimes/covid-19-data

Files:
------

1. usafull_final.csv
   - Dimensions: 3105 rows (US counties) x 299 columns
   - First column: row index
   - Second column: county ID
   - Third column: county population
   - Columns 4-299: days
   - Columns: x4, x5, ..., x299 — each represents a successive days
   - Values: integer cumulative COVID-19 case counts
   - Though cumulative counts, the rows are not monotonically increasing due to reporting errors and other discrepancies.
